[Requirement]
----------------------------------------------
|OS       | Windows 7 or later               |
|Graphics | DirectX 9.0c                     |
|Audio    | XAudio 2.0                       |
|Input    | DirectInput compatible controller|
----------------------------------------------

[Controller Configuration (Default)]
-----------------------------------
|Move           | Left Analog Key |
|Jump           | Button 0        |
|Start Button   | Button 7        |
|Menu Decide    | Button 0        |
|Menu Cancel    | Button 1        |
-----------------------------------

you can change gamepad assign on Title sequence.

[Keyboard Configuration]
------------------------------
|Move           | Arrow Keys  |
|Jump           | Space       |
|Start Button   | Return      |
|Menu Decide    | Space       |
|Menu Cancel    | Back Space  |
|Player Change  | Number Keys |
|Game Exit      | Esc         |
------------------------------
